# Use Cases for TaskFlow

## Scenario 1: Product Planning and Task Allocation
As a Product Manager at a software company, Emily often needs to oversee the planning of new features. She logs into TaskFlow and navigates to the project list. From there, she creates a new project that outlines all the features for the upcoming release. After creating the project, Emily allocates tasks to her development team using the easy-to-use interface. Each team member can now see their assigned tasks and deadlines, allowing them to understand their responsibilities clearly. Emily appreciates how TaskFlow helps her keep the team organized and on track.

## Scenario 2: Managing Team Workflows
David, a Business Unit Manager, is responsible for ensuring that his team's tasks align with the overall business objectives. He uses TaskFlow to review all ongoing projects and their respective tasks. By filtering the tasks by user, he quickly identifies any bottlenecks and reallocates resources as necessary. David enjoys how TaskFlow's straightforward interface allows him to stay productive and make informed decisions regarding task management, improving the overall efficiency of his unit.

## Scenario 3: Daily Task Tracking
As an End User and software developer, Sarah logs into TaskFlow each morning to review her tasks for the day. She accesses the task list, where she can see all her assignments, including key deadlines and priorities. If she needs clarification on a specific task, she can easily communicate through the application. At the end of the day, Sarah updates the status of her tasks, and with just a few clicks, she reports her progress back to her team lead. This streamlined process helps Sarah manage her time effectively and contribute to her project's success.

## Scenario 4: New User Registration and Authentication
John, a new team member, needs access to TaskFlow. He goes to the login page and registers for a new account. Using the intuitive registration form, he provides his details and sets up his password securely. After completing the registration process, he receives a confirmation email and logs into the application for the first time. With TaskFlow’s smooth authentication process, John can start accessing project information almost immediately, allowing him to integrate into the team quickly.

## Scenario 5: Collaborating with the AI Assistant
In her quest to brainstorm ideas for a new feature, Emily decides to use TaskFlow's integrated AI assistant. She navigates to the assistant component and starts a chat session. The AI helps her generate a list of potential feature ideas based on current user feedback and market trends. This collaborative use of TaskFlow enhances Emily’s creative process and equips her with valuable insights, making her feature planning more effective and user-focused.
