# Use Cases for eShopOnWeb

## Product Manager Scenario
As a Product Manager for eShopOnWeb, I need to identify the latest trends in catalog items to optimize our product offerings. I log into the admin dashboard and navigate to the catalog management section. Here, I can view the list of all catalog items along with their sales performance metrics. Using this data, I quickly identify underperforming items and create a report to recommend their removal or discounting. By streamlining our inventory based on these insights, I enhance the product lineup for our customers while maximizing revenue.

## Business Unit Scenario
As a manager of the sales team in a Business Unit, I rely on eShopOnWeb to understand customer preferences. Each morning, I check the dashboard for updates on recent product trends and customer purchase behaviors. By accessing the catalog data, I can see which items are popular and which brands are gaining traction. This information helps me tailor our promotional strategies and guide the sales team on what products they should emphasize in their outreach, ensuring we meet customer needs and drive sales.

## End User Scenario
As an end user shopping on eShopOnWeb, I look for the newest items available. After logging in, I use the search functionality to explore the latest catalog items. I easily filter results by category and brand, accessing detailed information about each product, including descriptions, prices, and availability. I add my favorite items to the shopping cart and can view my order history for quick reordering. This seamless experience allows me to efficiently find and purchase the products I love.

## Another End User Scenario
As a frequent buyer using eShopOnWeb, I often need to check the status of my orders. After logging into my account, I navigate to the "My Orders" section. Here, I can see all items I've purchased, their shipment statuses, and estimated delivery dates. If I need to make changes or contact support, I can access my order's details with just a couple of clicks. This transparency and ease of access empower me as a customer and make my online shopping experience notably pleasant.
