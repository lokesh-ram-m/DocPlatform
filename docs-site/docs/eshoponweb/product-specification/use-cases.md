# Use Cases for eShopOnWeb

## Product Manager Use Case: Managing Catalog Items
As a Product Manager, I want to ensure that our product catalog is updated with the latest offerings. Each week, I log into eShopOnWeb and navigate to the Catalog Management section using the BlazorAdmin interface. From there, I can view the list of existing catalog items, and if I identify any gaps, such as outdated product descriptions or missing items, I can create new catalog entries or update existing ones. This ensures that our customers always see the most relevant products, which in turn helps boost sales.

## Business Unit Use Case: Analyzing Sales Data
In my role within the Business Unit, I rely heavily on analytics to gauge the performance of our various product lines. I access eShopOnWeb and utilize the reporting tools available to examine sales figures and trends, segmented by product categories. This helps me make informed decisions about inventory management and promotional strategies. If I observe an upward trend in a specific product category, I can collaborate with the marketing team to launch targeted campaigns, ensuring we maximize sales potential.

## End User Use Case: Individual Shopping Experience
As an end user, I enjoy using eShopOnWeb to shop for my favorite brands. When I visit the site, I can easily browse through the catalog of items. If I find a product I like, I can click on it to view detailed descriptions and options. If I'm ready to purchase, I can add the item to my basket and proceed to checkout seamlessly. The intuitive design allows me to manage my account settings, and I appreciate the ability to review my past orders and manage returns directly through my account.

## Product Manager Use Case: Ensuring User Security
Safety and data protection are paramount in my role as a Product Manager. By utilizing the robust JWT authentication provided by eShopOnWeb, I ensure that our customers' data is secure during their shopping experience. Regular security audits help me monitor for potential vulnerabilities, and I can make swift decisions based on insights derived from user analytics. This proactive approach nurtures customer trust and fosters brand loyalty.

## Business Unit Use Case: Coordinating Promotions
As part of a business initiative, my goal is to launch a seasonal promotion for our products. Using eShopOnWeb, I can coordinate with the marketing team to update the catalog items to include discounts and special offers. I leverage the insight tools within eShopOnWeb to monitor the success of the promotion in real-time. This agility allows us to make data-driven decisions and adjustments, optimizing our marketing efforts as the campaign progresses.
