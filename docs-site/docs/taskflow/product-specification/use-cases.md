# Use Cases for TaskFlow

## Use Case 1: Product Manager Monitoring Project Progress
As a Product Manager, Sarah logs into TaskFlow to review the progress of projects under her supervision. She navigates to the project list section of the application, where she can see a comprehensive overview of all active projects. Sarah clicks on a specific project to see detailed information, including the tasks assigned to team members. This allows her to track timelines, identify bottlenecks, and adjust resources as needed to ensure that the project stays on track. With this insight, Sarah can effectively communicate updates and make informed decisions regarding project priorities.

## Use Case 2: Business Unit Leader Assigning Tasks
Mark, a leader in the marketing department, uses TaskFlow to delegate tasks for an upcoming campaign. He accesses the task list interface and creates new tasks, assigning them to specific team members based on their expertise and current workload. Mark sets deadlines and priorities for each task, ensuring clarity in expectations. After assigning the tasks, he can monitor their progress in real-time, allowing him to provide support where necessary and make adjustments to stay aligned with campaign goals. TaskFlow enables Mark to streamline his team’s workflow and efficiently manage the project.

## Use Case 3: End User Managing Personal Tasks
Jessica, a user of TaskFlow, opens the application to manage her personal to-do list. She logs in and is greeted by the task list, where she can view her existing tasks. Jessica adds new tasks for upcoming deadlines, setting reminders to keep herself organized. When she completes a task, she marks it as done, and TaskFlow updates her progress automatically. The user-friendly interface helps Jessica prioritize her tasks, allowing her to focus on what’s most important and stay productive throughout her day.

## Use Case 4: Authenticating and Registering New Users
As a new user interested in utilizing TaskFlow, David visits the application’s login page. Not yet having an account, he clicks on the registration link. After entering his details, including email and password, David submits the registration form. Upon confirmation of his account, he receives a welcome email. Now equipped with credentials, David logs into the application, providing him access to the robust features of TaskFlow. This simple and secure registration process enhances his onboarding experience, allowing him to dive straight into managing tasks efficiently.

## Use Case 5: AI-Powered Task Assistance
While working on a project, Mark wants to generate content for a report quickly. He decides to use the AI assistant feature within TaskFlow. Mark navigates to the assistant component and describes what he needs help with. The AI utilizes the Semantic Kernel capabilities to generate a draft for him, helping streamline his work. This interaction not only saves Mark time but also enhances the overall quality of his output, showcasing the added value of integrating AI within TaskFlow.
